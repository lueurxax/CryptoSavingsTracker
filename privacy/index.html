CryptoSavingsTracker Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how CryptoSavingsTracker handles information when you use the app.

Effective date: April 14, 2026

Overview

CryptoSavingsTracker is a savings goal and asset tracking app. The app is designed to help you create goals, add assets, review progress, and manage related information on your device.

Information You Enter

The app may store information that you choose to enter, such as:

  • goal names, targets, deadlines, and notes,
  • asset names, balances, and related details,
  • transaction records and planning data,
  • app settings and preferences.

This information is used only to provide the app’s functionality.

Storage

Your app data is primarily stored on your device. If you enable Apple sync or sharing features that are available in your version of the app, related data may also be processed through Apple services such as iCloud or CloudKit.

Permissions

  • Camera: used only for optional QR-based pairing or sync flows.
  • Local Network: used only for optional local device-to-device sync flows.
  • Notifications: used only if you choose to allow reminders or app notifications.

You can deny optional permissions and continue using the core app experience.

Third-Party and Apple Services

The app may use Apple frameworks and Apple cloud services when relevant features are enabled. The app may also contact third-party market or balance data providers when you use features that refresh external asset data.

CryptoSavingsTracker does not sell your personal data and is not designed for third-party advertising tracking.

Your Choices

You can control optional permissions through iOS Settings. You can also choose not to use optional sync, sharing, or camera-based flows.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the support page: CryptoSavingsTracker Support.
