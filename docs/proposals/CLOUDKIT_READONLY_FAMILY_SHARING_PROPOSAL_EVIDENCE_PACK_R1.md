## D. Assumptions and Open Questions
- ASSUMP-01: `Apple devices` in this proposal still means at least iPhone and iPad; macOS support for owner and invitee surfaces is not yet explicitly locked.
- ASSUMP-02: Xcode Preview evidence is sufficient for this review pass because the proposal-critical current-state surfaces are static and the feature does not yet exist in the runtime.
- ASSUMP-03: The first shipping cut will continue to keep CloudKit as the only authoritative store and use family sharing only as a read-only projection path.
- QUESTION-01: Does v1 include macOS invitee read-only surfaces, or is macOS explicitly deferred even though the current product already ships a macOS app?
- QUESTION-02: Is owner participant management intended to be system-first through `UICloudSharingController`, or is the team intentionally proposing a custom invite-management surface?
- QUESTION-03: What freshness SLA moves a shared goal from `active` to `stale`, and what user action should be available in `temporarilyUnavailable`?
