## D. Assumptions and Open Questions
- ASSUMP-01: Full-goal-set sharing remains a deliberate v1 simplification, not a placeholder for later goal-by-goal sharing.
- ASSUMP-02: The proposal's new atomic publish/outbox contract is intended to be normative, not optional implementation guidance.
- ASSUMP-03: Current-state preview evidence remains sufficient because the feature is still proposal-only and shell deltas are conceptual.
- QUESTION-01: Should the proposal replace the newly locked app-delegate acceptance bridge with a scene-based acceptance bridge to match Apple's current guidance for scene-based/SwiftUI apps?
- QUESTION-02: Are the quantitative kill-switch thresholds intentionally final, or are they placeholders pending real beta traffic?
- QUESTION-03: Does grouping by owner need a secondary sort/order rule when multiple owner datasets are present?
