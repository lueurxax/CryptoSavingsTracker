## D. Assumptions and Open Questions
- ASSUMP-01: Full-goal-set sharing remains the intended v1 product simplification and not a temporary placeholder.
- ASSUMP-02: The proposal's outbox/coordinator and scene-based acceptance model are normative implementation requirements.
- ASSUMP-03: Current-state previews remain sufficient because the feature does not yet exist in runtime code.
- QUESTION-01: Does the proposal still need explicit invitee success/empty states and revoke recovery details, or are they implied strongly enough by current lifecycle language?
- QUESTION-02: Is the existing material/contrast language sufficient for implementation, or does the design still need a fuller motion/system-material contract?
- QUESTION-03: Does the local cache schema evolution policy need to be specified before implementation, or is that reasonable follow-on work?
